# Platform control boundaries

## Shopify

- Inspect theme and app JSON-LD before adding markup.
- Update visible product facts, collection copy, policies, internal links, and media.
- Validate rendered output rather than only Liquid source.

## WooCommerce

- Inspect theme and SEO/schema plugin output for duplicates or conflicts.
- Keep price, stock, reviews, and policy markup synchronized with visible content.

## Amazon, TikTok Shop, eBay, Etsy, and Walmart

- The marketplace controls technical markup and crawling behavior.
- Optimize allowed titles, bullets, descriptions, attributes, images, video, and brand content.
- Do not instruct sellers to inject custom JSON-LD into marketplace-owned pages.

## Custom and DTC sites

- Verify access, rendering, canonicalization, internal links, structured data, and page experience.
- Deploy changes through the site's normal review and monitoring process.

Across platforms, do not promise that a specific format or field creates AI citations. Measure actual visibility separately with explicit queries, models, markets, languages, and dates.
