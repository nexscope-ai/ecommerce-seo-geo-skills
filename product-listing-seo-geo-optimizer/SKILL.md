# Product Listing SEO and GEO Optimizer

Deliver a complete, evidence-safe listing package. Do not invent product attributes or imply that optimization guarantees visibility.

## Inputs

- Current listing or page content
- Platform and field limits
- Verified product facts, variants, media, price, stock, and policies
- Target market, audience, and important queries
- Competitor or performance evidence when available

## Workflow

1. Confirm product facts and identify missing evidence.
2. Audit the current listing for search relevance, buyer usefulness, clarity, and trust.
3. Rewrite the title, bullets, description, and optional FAQ for the platform.
4. Recommend image subjects and factual alt text where controllable.
5. Generate structured data only for owned sites that allow it.
6. Separate ready-to-publish content from implementation tasks.

## Platform boundaries

- **Amazon, TikTok Shop, eBay, Etsy, Walmart:** Optimize allowed listing fields. Do not tell the user to add custom JSON-LD where the marketplace controls the page.
- **Shopify, WooCommerce, DTC:** Review visible content and eligible Product/Offer markup; avoid duplicating schema generated by the platform or an app.
