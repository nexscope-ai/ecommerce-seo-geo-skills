# Ecommerce GEO audit checklist

Use only the sections supported by the available evidence.

## Evidence and scope

- Record URL, page type, platform, market, language, and audit date.
- Record whether rendered HTML, source, crawl, screenshots, structured data, or performance data were inspected.
- List every material unknown.

## Blockers

- Access or indexability problem confirmed
- Missing or contradictory product identity
- Important facts unavailable in text
- Invalid or misleading structured data
- Price, stock, review, or policy mismatch
- Marketplace limitation incorrectly treated as an owned-site fix

## Visible content

- Product, brand, category, audience, and primary use cases are clear.
- Specifications, compatibility, variants, limitations, and policies are accurate.
- Copy answers genuine buyer questions without unsupported claims.
- Images and media have useful text alternatives where needed.

## Trust

- Seller and contact identity are clear.
- Reviews and ratings are genuine and consistent.
- Warranty, returns, shipping, safety, and certifications are verifiable.

## Measurement

- Readiness findings are not described as actual AI visibility.
- Actual AI-answer evidence includes query, model, market, language, and date.
- Recommendations include a validation method and owner.
- No unsupported traffic, ranking, citation, conversion, or sales forecast is included.
